UB ACM
Building the CSE community
UB ACM is the SUNY Buffalo student chapter of the Association for Computing Machinery. We organize numerous academic and social events for the UB CSE community.

Messages

9/4/2014
We would like to thank everyone who came to our General Interest meeting yesterday. The turnout was phenomenal! Topics discussed at the meeting include the upcoming intern panel, mock interview nights, hack mights, and ideas for some off-campus events. We also announced UB Hacking, which is on the calendar for the weekend of November 7th to November 9th. The last topic we discussed, which is new to ACM but we have high hopes for, is a starting a mentorship program that would pair freshman and sophomore students with upper level classmen. More details will be announced later.
